Learn About Gene Hackman and Wife Betsy Arakawa

 Posted on May 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  493 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

What a talented couple! Longtime actor Gene Hackman and his second wife, retired classical pianist Betsy Arakawa, got married in 1991 and have been going strong ever since.

What brought Gene Hackman and Betsy Arakawa together?

According to multiple sources, the Royal Tenenbaums actor and the Hawaii native met at a Los Angeles fitness center where Betsy worked part-time. They began dating in 1984, and Betsy moved into Gene’s Santa Fe, New Mexico, home.

Gene’s divorce from his first wife, Faye Maltese, was still pending at the time. Faye, who died in 2017, was married to the Academy Award winner from 1956 to 1986. Leslie Anne, Elizabeth Jean, and Christopher Allen were the couple’s three children.

Are Gene Hackman and Betsy Arakawa parents?

Despite the fact that Gene and Betsy never had children of their own, the San Bernardino, California, native remains close to Leslie, Elizabeth, and Christopher. “He wishes he’d been there more for his children, but he’s now close with them and their children,” a source previously told Closer Weekly.

Gene’s acting career did, admittedly, make him somewhat of an absent father.

“I lost touch with my son early on in terms of advice.” “I was doing location films at a time when he needed support and guidance,” Gene explained in a 2011 interview with GQ magazine.

What happened to Gene Hackman and Betsy Arakawa today?

In 2004, Gene quietly left Hollywood.

“I haven’t held a press conference to announce my retirement, but yes, I’m no longer going to act,” Gene later revealed in an interview in 2008. “I miss the acting part because that’s what I did for almost 60 years and I really enjoyed it.” But the business is extremely stressful for me.”

He and Betsy have been living a quiet life together since then.

“His health is good, he still bicycles, does yard work, and he’s a great handyman,” a source told Closer Weekly. “After all of Gene’s career drama, he enjoys the peaceful life he shares with the lovely Betsy.”
