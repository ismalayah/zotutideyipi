All About Joan Sebastians Children and Their Battle For Inheritance

 Posted on May 24, 2024  |  17 minutes  |  3487 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Joan Sebastian’s children are eight from five different relationships. He had five sons named José Manuel Figueroa, Trigo de Jesús Figueroa, Juan Sebastián Figueroa, Julian Figueroa, and Zarelea Figueroa, as well as three daughters named Marcelia Figueroa, Juliana Figueroa, and D’Yave Figueroa.

Prior to his demise in 2015, Mexican singer and songwriter, Joan Sebastian, enjoyed a four-decade-long music career that saw him reportedly compose more than a thousand songs. Before embarking on that glittering music career, the Mexican songwriter had initially trained to be a Catholic priest but ultimately ended up becoming a father to eight children. Two of those children died before him, and another recently joined him in the afterlife.

A significant glare of the spotlight landed on Joan Sebastian’s kids after his death because of the battle for his inheritance that followed, a battle that commenced because the singer did not leave any will behind. Here’s what we know about Joan’s children and their battle for inheritance.

Joan Sebastian’s Children Are From Five Different Women

Born Jose Manuel Figueroa Sr. on April 8, 1951, in Juliantla, Guerrero, Mexico, Joan Sebastian had an expansive love life that often led to romantic relationships with several women including the five women that bore his eight children. He had his first three children, Jose Manuel Figueroa Gonzalez, Juan Sebastian Figueroa Gonzalez, and Trigo de Jesus Figueroa Gonzalez, from his marriage to his first wife Teresa Gonzalez.

Not much is known about Teresa except that she was reportedly married to the late songwriter for quite a while from 1973 to 1986. Not long after his marriage to Teresa ended, Joan embarked on another romantic dalliance with actress Maria del Carmen Ocampo. That relationship eventually led to the birth of his first daughter, Zaralea Figueroa Ocampo.

In 1992, Joan Sebastian exchanged marital vows with Maribel Guardia. Guardia was a model and beauty queen best known for winning the Miss Costa Rica beauty pageant in 1978. She was also quite involved in the movie industry as an actress and was married to the late musician for about four years until their divorce in 1996. However, their time as a married couple led to the birth of a son named Jose Julian Figueroa Guardia.

We have yet to ascertain exactly when Joan Sebastian married his third wife Alina Espin but we can confirm that the Mexican woman of whom very little is known managed to give Sebastian two more children in the form of daughters Joana Marcelia Figueroa Espin, and D’Yave Figueroa Espin.

The talented songwriter welcomed his youngest child, a daughter named Juliana Joeri Figueroa Alonso, from his 12-year relationship with her mother Erica Alonso. Despite the significant number of years that they were together, Joan never married her.

Jose Manuel Figueroa Gonzalez was born on May 15, 1975, in an unnamed health facility in Chicago, Illinois, United States. However, given his ancestry, he can also claim his Mexican heritage through his parents.

Speaking of parents, Jose Manuel is the oldest child of the late singer Joan Sebastian whom he had from his first-ever marriage to fellow Mexican Teresa Gonzalez. Their marital union, if several sources on the matter are proven accurate, lasted for about thirteen years from 1973 to 1986. Those dates further confirm the fact that Jose Manuel was barely a teenager when his parents split up.

Nonetheless, the singer spent his formative years in Chicago but the details of his formal educational background have yet to become public knowledge.

Jose Manuel is also in Show Business

Jose Manuel Figueroa Gonzalez is the perfect illustration of the fact that the apple doesn’t fall far from the tree. That premise is well-established in the fact that he actively earns a living primarily from following in his father’s footsteps as a singer. His interest in the world of music began at a very young age, and he honed the skills that came with that interest to a fine edge with the support and guidance of his late father.

His impact in the world of music has also been keenly felt as he has been touted as the singer responsible for rebranding the ranchera musical genre with the aid of unique musical arrangements.

It is worth recognizing the fact Joan Sebastian’s oldest son signed his first recording deal with Fonovisia record label in 1994, when he was just a 19-year-old teenager before releasing his debut album titled Expulsado del Paraiso the very next year in 1995. The album received positive reviews and has sold more than 30 million copies globally.

He did not rest on the laurels of his first album, and his dedication and hard work were made evident in the fact that he went on to release several other albums including Jose Manuel Figueroa (1998), Mala Hierba (1999), A Caballo (2000), Immortal (2004), Rosas y Espinas (2013), No Estas Tu (2017), and most recently Paso a Pasito (2022).

While he actively represents his family legacy in the world of music, Jose Manuel’s sphere of influence has spread to include active involvement as an actor.m he was chosen to portray his late father in his latter years in the TV series Por Siempre Joan Sebastian, a series that was dedicated to the life and memory of his father.

Before he participated in the series which premiered on June 27, 2016, he had been involved, as an actor, in the production of several other TV series including La hora pico (2000) and Todo Incluido (2013).

Jose Manuel Figueroa Gonzalez is a Father of Two

Jose Manuel prefers to keep details of his personal life away from the media and the public, and as such, there is very little information about his love life including whether he is married or in any committed romantic relationship. According to several sources though, the talented musician is a father of two children namely Gabriela and Nikolas.

However, we have yet to ascertain the identity or identities, depending on whether there is more than one woman responsible for the birth of those two children.

Juan Sebastian Figueroa Gonzalez is the second son of Joan Sebastian. Chronologically, he is also the late singer’s second child and the second child he had with his first wife Teresa Gonzalez. Joan’s second oldest child was born on August 17, 1977, around four years after his parents had officially become man and wife. The end of his parents’ marital union did not prevent them from providing the best quality of life for Juan including in the areas of his academics.

However, the details of his academic achievements have yet to see the light of day, and one is left to speculate on the matter. The same theory applies to what he did for a living even though he was most likely in show business.

Juan Sebastian was Murdered in 2010

Juan Sebastian is one of two of Joan Sebastian’s children who died before him. It didn’t help that the circumstances surrounding his demise were as brutal as they come. Juan was shot and killed in Cuernavaca, Morelos, Mexico, on June 12, 2010. The reports about his murder claimed that he had been denied entrance into a nightclub and that the melee that followed ultimately led to him being shot, and subsequently dying from the bullet wounds.

Given his high-profile family, the news of his death quickly spread like wildfire with several speculations about the people responsible for his murder not the least of which was that the Mexican drug cartel was the culprit. It was a speculation that did not hold much water and was further discredited by the deceased’s father who revealed that the cartel had nothing to do with the death of his son.

Juan’s personal life never saw the light of day, and it was difficult to determine if he was married or single. The late celebrity child was only 32 years old when he met his unfortunate end.

Trigo de Jesus Figueroa Gonzalez was born on June 25, 1979, around six years after his parents’ marriage to each other. Trigo was educated but the extent of his academic background has yet to be determined. In terms of what he did for a living, it was said that the late singer’s equally late son did not share the musical talent that ran in the family but that such simple matters did not prevent him from being involved in the music industry.

According to several sources, he served as one of his father’s collaborators and was often tasked with going with him on tours, ensuring that everything was in order, especially security, and generally just being there for his father when needed.

Trigo de Jesus Figueroa was also Shot Dead

Trigo de Jesus Figueroa became the first of Joan Sebastian’s children to kick the bucket after he was also brutally murdered by gunshot in 2006. According to the reports, he had followed his dad on one of his many trips, and after his performance at Mission, Hidalgo County, in Texas, in the United States on August 27, 2006, the crowd became rowdy, and it fell on Trigo to ensure calm.

However, the methods he employed to carry out his duty did not meet the approval of some members of the crowd, and they reacted by pulling their guns to fire into the crowd. The sound of the gunshots reignited the pandemonium with everyone trying to find a safe place to escape to.

Trigo was quite unlucky in his attempt because one of the men carrying the gun hunted him down and delivered two gunshots to the back of his head. He was taken to McAllen Medical Center but succumbed to the wounds he sustained from the gunshot shortly after he arrived at the medical facility.

Trigo de Jesus was buried at Juliantla, Taxco de Alarcón Municipality, Guerrero, Mexico.

Joan Sebastian Died in 2015

Joan Sebastian succumbed to bone cancer on July 13, 2015, at his ranch in Cruz de la Sierra, Teacalco Guerrero, Mexico. He was buried in a cemetery in Juliantla near his son Trigo.

The singer was first diagnosed with the disease in 1999, and by the end of his life, he had engaged in four battles with the disease but lost on the fourth occasion. He left behind an estate that included his $5 million net worth and 51 properties in different Mexican states.

Some of these properties have fallen into disrepair, according to the family lawyer, but this is an assertion that has been rejected by the singer’s youngest daughter Juliana who claims that her father has other assets and bank accounts which has yet to be revealed.

She further accused her older brothers of trying to cheat her out of what is due to her stating that they should not bank on the fact that she lives in relative comfort to deny her what is rightfully hers as she would utilize every legal means to regain what was rightfully hers.

All of Joan Sebastian’s Eight Children were Declared Heirs To his Fortune

Since he did not leave a will that would have expressed his desires concerning his estate, Joan Sebastian’s children have been at odds with one another about who should inherit his estate. That battle rages on and there appears to be no end in sight.

At the time of his death, six of the singer’s eight children were still alive, and the family lawyer Cipriano Sotelo explained that those surviving children would be the singer’s heir and also stated that the children of Trigo and Juan would get what was supposed to go to their deceased parents.

There have also been suggestions that the best way to divide the inheritance of Joan Sebastian among his children would be to sell off some of the properties and divide the monies realized equally among his heirs.
