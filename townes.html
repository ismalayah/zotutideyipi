Townes, Hoyt, and Miles (Japanese: レモンキッズ Lemon Kids) are minor characters who appeared in The Power of Us. The three of them run a lemonade stand in Fula City.

History

Townes, Hoyt, and Miles were first seen running a lemonade stand in Fula City. While moving their operation, Margo bumped into them and they lost several of their lemons. Annoyed, they threw a lemon at her, only for Ash to catch it. They sent Phanpy out to battle Pikachu, only for it to be quickly defeated. Ash asked if they were interested in a second round, but the three boys left in a hurry.

Townes, Hoyt, and Miles were later seen helping the other civilians clean up the city. They informed Risa and the others about Zeraora's curse, but Margo dismissed their tale as fake.

Character

Townes, Hoyt, and Miles are a boisterous group of boys that often hang out together. The trio enjoys a good laugh and they often discuss the strange rumors surrounding Fula City.

On hand (Hoyt)

Phanpy
Phanpy is Hoyt's only known Pokémon. It was present as they carted around their lemon stand. Phanpy was used to battle Ash, going up against Pikachu. Hoyt was initially confident of his victory, but Pikachu quickly dodged Phanpy's Rollout which caused Phanpy to roll into Miles because of its extra momentum. A follow-up Iron Tail knocked both Phanpy and Miles out, ending the match.

Phanpy's only known moves are Rollout and Tackle.
